hi nice to meetcha

Alen is a 19-year-old girl, born and raised in the Philippines her interest is in digital art (Photography, cinematography, Graphic Design, Web Design), Science, Fashion and soulful chill music. Alen is taking up a Bachelor's Degree Major in Information Technology at Rizal Technological University she is now in her sophomore year..

Alen loves and enjoys drinking iced coffee because she believes that her body is in a good state when she is drinking iced coffee. she also enjoys capturing memories, Moments which we cannot ever return to, but we can capture them up in the form of photos. Photographs are free passes to our past!!!

Alen is into Fashion, her style is Modern Retro which is combining the style and elegance of years gone by with our nifty modern fabrics and prints.

Usually, the long list of awards and achievements start here, but if we're being real here, she wasn't really keeping track.
